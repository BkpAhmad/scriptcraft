FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before placing your order

Is my order kept completely confidential?
+
Absolutely. We have a strict confidentiality policy. Your personal information and order details are never shared with third parties. All writers sign NDA agreements. Your order is between you and your assigned writer only.
How do you ensure originality / no plagiarism?
+
Every document is written from scratch specifically for your order. Before delivery, all papers are scanned through our plagiarism detection system. We provide a plagiarism report on request. Each order is unique — we never resell or reuse papers.
What if I need revisions?
+
Every order includes 2 free revisions within 7 days of delivery. If the paper doesn't match your original instructions, we revise it at no extra cost. Additional revisions beyond the free ones are available at a small fee.
What's the shortest deadline you can handle?
+
Our shortest deadline is 3 hours for urgent orders up to 5 pages. For longer papers, we recommend at least 12-24 hours. Rush orders are subject to a urgency surcharge. We always aim to deliver before the deadline so you have time to review.
Which citation styles do you support?
+
We support all major citation formats including APA (7th edition), MLA (9th edition), Chicago/Turabian, Harvard, and IEEE. Just specify your required format in the order form and our writers will follow it precisely.
Can I communicate with my assigned writer?
+
Yes. Once a writer is assigned to your order, you can communicate with them directly through our messaging system. You can share additional instructions, answer writer questions, and track progress — all within your order dashboard.
What payment methods do you accept?
+
We accept all major credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex), PayPal, and Apple Pay. All payments are processed securely through Stripe with 256-bit SSL encryption. We never store your payment details.
